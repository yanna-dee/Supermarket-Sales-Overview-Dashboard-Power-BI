## Project Summary
This project presents an interactive Sales Overview Dashboard built in Power BI, designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, customer satisfaction, and operational trends for a Myanmar-based supermarket in Q1 2019.

The dashboard converts raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights, supporting data-driven decision-making across key business functions such as sales, marketing, and operations.

## Business Objective
To provide a centralized view of key sales metrics and answer critical business questions:
- Which customer segments drive the most revenue?
- What product categories perform best?
- Are there noticeable sales trends over time?
- How do customer behaviors vary by location and payment method?

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: $323K
- Gross Profit: $15K
- COGS: $308K
- Orders: 1,000
- Avg. Quantity per Order: 6
- Avg. Customer Rating: 7.0 (highest rating is 10.0)

## Key Insights and Analysis
### Member vs Non-Members
- Members contribute 59% of total sales, indicating stronger value from loyal customers
- Insight: Opportunity to expand loyalty programs and retention strategies

### Customer Rating
- Average Customer Rating is 7.0
- Insight: Investigate drivers of lower ratings (e.g., product quality, merchandising, demand alignment, customer service) to improve overall customer satisfaction

### Product Line Performance
- Food & Beverages and Sports & Travel are top-performing categories
- Insight: Prioritize inventory and promotions for high-performing categories, while optimizing product mix to support and uplift underperforming items

### Sales Trend
- Sales declined in February before recovering in March
- Insight: This may indicate seasonality or external factors; further analysis is needed to identify root causes, prevent similar declines, and sustain growth in future periods.

### Geographic Insights
- Sales are relatively evenly distributed across cities, with Naypyitaw slightly leading
- Insight: Balanced regional demand suggests opportunities for scalable growth across locations

### Payment Behavior
- Cash is the most preferred payment method, followed closely by e-wallets
- Insight: Digital payment adoption is strong—ensure systems remain reliable to prevent transaction failures and improve customer experience

## Dashboard Features
